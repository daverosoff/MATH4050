Definition 54.

Let \(C(\Q)\) be the set of all Cauchy sequences of rational numbers and define a relation \(\sim\) on \(C(\Q)\) by \(a_n \sim b_n\) if, for all rational \(\eps \gt 0\text{,}\) there is \(N \in \N\) such that \(\abs{a_n - b_n} \lt \eps\) for all \(n \geq N\text{.}\)
